I Qualifications
  1. The Applicant shall be a company or group that is legally registered and has the independent capacity for civil liability and shall have the valid operating qualification certificates or equivalent proof in the country/region where it is registered, for example, a valid business license or Form W-9 in the USA.
  2. The Applicant is able to provide the invoices with the name consistent with what is listed in the Business License and sign contracts independently under such entity name (the subject shall not be changed during the recruitmentperiod).
  3. The Applicant shall be in good faith and be in compliance with the applicable laws and regulations.
  4. The Applicant must guarantee that the contents provided are legal and shall bear the relevant legal liabilities caused thereby.
  5. The Applicant must guarantee that it is the direct author of the contents provided and that it has stable audio making teams.
  6. If Tencent’s former employee is the legal person or holds any office in the management, his/her dismissal period must not be less than 18 months.

II Commercial Requirements
  1. The Applicant shall have its dedicated business team for business communication, with the members having the experience in the music or audio industry and basic business judgment ability.
  2. The Applicant’s organizational structure is well defined. The investment relation, for example, whether it is an affiliate, shall be cleared. The Tencent support team, if any, may be clearly specified, so that the corresponding materials can be provided upon formal cooperation.
  3. The Applicant shall have regular management mechanism in place and clearly specify the procedures for getting on board/leaving, confidentiality system, internal training, demand response and emergency response mechanism, etc. and make available the same to Tencent for filing upon formal cooperation.
III Technical Requirements
  1.  The Applicant shall have strict technical review mechanisms and procedures in place.
  2.  Technical Response: to be responded to within 2 hours during working days; and to be responded to within 4 hours during non-working days. In case of leaves or holidays, advanced instructions and back-up plans shall be made. Any staff changes/transfers shall be notified at least one month in advance.
